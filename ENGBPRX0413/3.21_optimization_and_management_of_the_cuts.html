3.21 OPTIMIZATION AND MANAGEMENT OF THE CUTS

    Through the third button in the top right, you access the optimization and cut management screen.

    Lower bar

    In the lower section are shown some buttons that allow to perform operations on cuts.

    Buttons

    Probing Slab
    Allows to add a slab probe cycle before executing the cuts.

    Create ISO program
    Generate the cutting program and allows to access the ISO control page.

    Manual move
    Allows to move a section of material using the suction cups.

    Move automatic.
    Performs the material shifts needed to not ruin any piece.

    Multiple selection
    Allows to select more cuts at the time.

    Single cut
    Allows to add one cut manually to the slab.

    Bench washing
    Allows to start a washing cycle of an area, or to wash the entire work bench.

    Operations on cuts
    Allows to apply operations and/or custom orderings on the cuts.
