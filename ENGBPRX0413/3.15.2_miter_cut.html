3.15.2 MITER CUT

    Through this function, the operator can incline a side of the selected piece.

    Buttons

    Team direction positive
    Allows the operator to create a cut with positive inclination of value indicated in the text box below.

    Negative team direction
    Allows the operator to create a cut with a negative inclination of value indicated in the text box below.

    Execution of working

    We apply a _45_ miter cut to the selected piece.
    You select the button with the _ and you insert the number 45 in the box below.

    You go to select the side of the piece to which this miter cut will be applied, once selected the miter cut is created automatically.

    Warning

    WARNING!This modification does not apply to the pieces of the list belonging to the same row of the modified piece.

    Rapid controls

    By clicking with the right mouse button on the cut of a piece inside the working area, a window appears with commands that allow you to modify its inclination.

    Warning

    ATTENTION!These buttons allow exclusively the inclination of the side respectively at 45 and -45_.
