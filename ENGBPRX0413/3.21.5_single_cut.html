3.21.5 SINGLE CUT

    This functionality allows to insert manually linear cuts with disk on the work bench.

    Buttons

    Directional Move
    Move the selected vertex in one of the directions.
    In the text box below it is possible to change the distance of the move.

    Delete
    Allows to remove the cut and its vertices from the work area before confirmation.

    Horizontal Cut
    Allows to insert a cut arranged horizontally.

    Cut in vertical
    Allows to insert a cut arranged vertically.

    Execution of the functionality

    After having pressed the button, the program will ask the user to select on the working area the starting and ending point of the cut that you want to add.

    It is possible to change the position of the ends of the selected cut by selecting them and dragging them on the working area, or using the arrows for directional movement.

    The operator can impose that the resulting cut must be arranged horizontally or vertically.

    At confirmation, the new cut is added at the end of the list.
