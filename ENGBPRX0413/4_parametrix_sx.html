4 PARAMETRIX SX

    Parametrix has a version designed specifically to facilitate the workflow on machines belonging to the SX category.
    In particular, it allows the operator to manage efficiently the cut combinations, so to optimize its production process.
    In this chapter are described functionalities that differ from the base version of Parametrix.

    Side section

    The buttons located in the top right allow the operator to change the Parametrix screen.

    Each screen is responsible for a particular aspect of the processing of a slab.

    Pieces positioning
    Main screen of Parametrix.
    Operations of:

    • creation and modification of the pieces

    • acquisition of the slab photo on the bench and its perimeter;

    • positioning of the pieces on the work area

    Optimization and cut management
    Screen for optimize and cut management.
    Operations of:

    • handling of the material with vacuum

    • bench washing

    • modification of cuts to optimize their combinations;

    Combination of the cuts
    Final screen of the workflow.
    Operations of:

    • viewing and sorting of the combined cuts;

    • management of the unloader

    • start of the program of the machine of the cut;
