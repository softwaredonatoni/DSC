3.11.7 COMMERCIAL PIECE CREATION

    In this section of the 'Create pieces' panel it is possible to create the commercial pieces. The characteristics of this type of pieces are the following:

    • they are created with a high number of copies;

    • the dimensions of width and height are changeable even when a piece is inserted in the working area.

    Parameters

    Width
    Width of the rectangle. It is measured with the unit of measurement reported to its right.

    Height
    Height of the rectangle. It is measured with the unit of measure reported to its right.

    Mark
    Label that is added to piece to identify it. It is an optional parameter, as not fundamental for piece creation.

    Import of a commercial piece

    To correctly conclude the creation of a commercial piece, it’s necessary to use the following button:

    Import pieces commercial
    Confirm the creation of the commercial piece and insert it into the list of pieces to work.
