3.6 LASER MANAGEMENT

    This panel allows to add or remove laser references on the working area.

    Buttons

    Laser reference to line
    Allows to add on the work area the reference of the laser to line based on the position of the machine.

    Draws laser cross
    Allows to add on the working area the reference of the laser cross according to the position of the machine.

    Delete all drawings
    All lines and crosses inserted on the working area in this mode will be deleted.

    Delete a single drawing
    Allows the operator to delete a laser element drawn on the work area by clicking on it.

    Laser to line

    The line laser is a tool aligned with the disk mounted on the machine. It is used to collect information related to the position and the orientation of the disk on the bench.

    To acquire the reference, bring the machine to the desired point, and press the button to detect the direction of the laser at line and add the reference on the working area.
    Using more references, it is possible to recognize the perimeter and the position of the slab or of a piece on the work bench.

    Info

    NOTEThere are no limits on the number of references that is possible to save.

    Laser cross

    The laser cross is used to collect information related to the position of the laser pointer on the bench.

    To acquire the reference, bring the machine to the desired point, and press the button to detect the position of the laser cross and add the reference to the work area.
    Using more references, is possible to recognize the perimeter and the position of the slab or of a piece on the working bench.

    Info

    NOTEThere are no limits on the number of references that is possible to save.
