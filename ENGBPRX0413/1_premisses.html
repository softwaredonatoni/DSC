    Purpose of the manual

    This manual reports the use instructions necessary for the good knowledge of the Parametrix program and useful information to find solutions to malfunctions.
    From the observance of what is described in this manual depends the regular operation, the duration and the economy of operation of the machine itself.
    The machine usage and maintenance manual and this software manual must always be available at the plant installation location, in a safe and easily accessible place. If one of them is lost, contact the prevention and protection service manager.
    Failure to observe what is described in this manual, negligence, a bad and inadequate use of the machine, can be cause of cancellation, by the Manufacturing Company, of the warranty that it gives to the machine itself, and moreover it is possible not to obtain the advantages for which this machine has been built.
    DONATONI MACCHINE SRL, is still available to ensure prompt and accurate technical assistance and everything that can be useful for the best functioning and obtain the maximum of the yield from the machine.
    The Manufacturer reserves the right to modify, at its discretion, this publication and the data contained therein if deemed necessary for a technical or commercial improvement of the product.

    Preservation of the manual

    The present Software Manual must be kept for the entire life operating of the machine, even in case of cession to third.
    In order to correctly save the Software Manual it is recommended:

    • Employ the Manual in such a way as not to damage all, or in part, the content; in particular it is recommended not to leave the Manual during use and to place it in the assigned location immediately after the end of the consultation;

    • Do not remove, tear off or rewrite for any reason the parts of the Manual;

    • Keep the Manual in zones protected from humidity, heat and other environmental agents that could compromise its integrity or duration;

    • In case of loss or damage of all or part of the contents of the present Manual, require copy of such documentation to the Builder.
