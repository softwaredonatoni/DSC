3.21.6 WASHING CYCLE

    It is possible to start manually a cycle of washing of the area, enable the automatic adding of the entire bench and select a portion in which to execute the cleaning.

    Parameters

    Width
    It is displayed how much measures in width the area in creation phase.

    Height
    Is visualized what measures in height the area in creation phase.

    Create area
    Create an area that the operator can resize and move based on where he wants to perform the washing.

    Move
    The operator can move the area during the creation phase.

    Piece washing
    Create a piece washing area slightly upper than a selected piece.

    Clean bench
    The entire area of the bench will be affected by a washing cycle.

    Automatic washing cycle
    Automatic addition of a 'bench cleaning' phase at the end of the cutting list.
    The addition will happen at the passage to the cut window.

    Mode of insert a washing cycle

    The addition of a washing cycle involves the insertion, inside the work area, of a rectangle that indicates the portion of the bench subject to washing.
    It is possible to insert more washing cycles; the respective areas can also overlap, partially or completely.
    Each new washing cycle will be inserted at the end of the cuts list on the right side.

    Manual selection of the area

    By pressing the button, you enable the possibility to add customizable washing areas.
    It is therefore possible to move and resize the area.

    After having modified as you want the area for washing cycle, it is possible to add an additional area to be washed by pressing again the button.
