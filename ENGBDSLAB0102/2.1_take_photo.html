2.1 TAKE PHOTO

    This function imports the image of a slab via a camera connected to the device.
    At opening the panel the following screen will be displayed, initially without a preview image

    Preview photo

    The preview photo is the frame dominating the central and left part of the panel.
    It is possible to load the preview of the currently loaded image by pressing the 'Take picture' button.

    Parameters

    The parameters section is located at the top right of the panel and contains the following types of parameters:

    Main parameters

    NOTE

    The symbol '*' next to the names indicates that the parameter is mandatory

    Name
    Unique identifier of the Slab.

    Thickness slab
    Real thickness of the slab.

    Total thickness
    Total thickness of the slab.

    Material
    Type of stone associated with the slab.

    Finishing
    Surface treatment of the slab.

    Context Parameters

    Block
    Slab origin code.

    Supplier
    Company that supplied the slab.

    Pack
    Logistics and commercial grouping.

    Parameters buttons

    Some parameters feature appropriate buttons on the right side enabling the user to perform specific actions:

    Lock/Unlock parameter
    Indicates whether the parameter is respectively locked and unmodifiable, or unlocked and modifiable.
    It is possible to toggle the block and unblock of the parameter by clicking this button.

    Select
    Allows setting the parameter by selecting from the values previously entered in the system.

    Take photo action

    Take photo
    Pressing this button, the user proceeds to take a photo with the properly configured and system-connected camera.
    The taken photo will be displayed in Photo Preview.

    Warning!

    It is not possible to take a photo without first configuring all mandatory parameters.

    Lower bar

    Confirm
    After taking the picture and filling the parameters, confirm to open the window allowing to add or modify other parameters.

    Warning!

    Clicking on this confirm, the sheet is not added to the system yet; it will be necessary to do so in the next window.

    Cancel
    Closes the window without applying changes and returns to the Home screen.
