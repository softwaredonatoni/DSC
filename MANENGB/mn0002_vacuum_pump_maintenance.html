MACHINE OPERATOR

SAFETY REQUIREMENTS

Start work with the machine switched off, after cutting out the electrical, hydraulic and pneumatic energy sources, and with the emergency button pressed.
After power is cut off, wait at least 10 minutes before accessing the machine

Required PPE (mandatory)

The level of cleanliness of the water in the water system very much affects the good condition of the vacuum pump. The purchaser is responsible for ensuring that the features of the water used comply with the specifications of the vacuum pump given in the Instruction manual for operation and maintenance provided by the manufacturer.
Clean the tank, in other words remove all sediments that have built up on the bottom, than fill with clean water.
For more detailed information on the maintenance of the vacuum pump, refer to the Instruction manual for operation and maintenance provided by the manufacturer (in the attached documents) of the VACUUM PUMP.
